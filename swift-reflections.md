
12/03/2026: In terms of ethics and AI, I think my personal stance holds very little weight. As AI becomes exponentially more advance and prevalent, the race against it becomes less and less level. It's a losing battle. It's AI bringing not just a gun, but a gun and a world of knowledge and tactics, to a knife fight. I think the only way forward is to co-exist with this omnipresent entity. If I had to give my opinion - I'm saddened by the idea of wholly human created art being obsolete. I do however understand this could simply be the fear of change. I imagine with the invention of the printing press, of computers, and the internet there were sceptics. Now these technologies are so intertwined with our lives and are that we couldn't imagine or produce either without them. Maybe there is potential for new art forms here. For new creation. For us to use AI as a aid, colleague, catalyst for something new. Something amazing. That is my hope. I will continue to educate myself around uses for this new tool, even if I'm sceptical, so that I am not left behind if in a few years we cannot imagine art without the use of AI.
